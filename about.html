About

This site was written for people who tried sincerely and were told the failure was theirs.

It exists because many explanations around manifestation and positive thinking ignore how anxiety and the nervous system actually work, and place responsibility on individuals when the model itself does not apply.

Nothing here is meant to motivate, optimise, or fix you. It is meant to explain what happened so that pressure can stop and self-blame can dissolve.
